The Legacy of Venus Fleur

Crafting
Eternity

Born in the heart of Dubai, Venus Fleur was founded on a simple yet profound belief: that luxury gifting should be as enduring as the moments it celebrates.

A Vision of Timeless Elegance

In a city that moves at the speed of light, we chose to focus on what lasts. Venus Fleur is more than a florist; we are a luxury floral atelier dedicated to the art of preservation. Our journey began with a mission to source the world's most exquisite roses and transform them into eternal masterpieces.

Today, we serve Dubai's most prestigious districts—from the executive offices of DIFC to the private villas of Palm Jumeirah. Every box that leaves our studio is a testament to our commitment to quality, sophistication, and the Dubai spirit of excellence.

Uncompromising Quality

We only select the top 1% of roses from the finest gardens, ensuring every petal meets our rigorous standards of beauty.

Artisanal Craft

Each arrangement is handcrafted by master florists in our Dubai studio, combining traditional techniques with modern luxury design.

Sustainable Luxury

Our preserved roses are an eco-conscious choice, reducing waste while providing a year-long floral experience without water.

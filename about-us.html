Our Vision: The Dubai Gold Standard

Established in 2021, Venus Fleur was founded to answer a specific challenge: how to provide enduring floral luxury in the demanding climate of the UAE. Traditional bouquets, while fleetingly beautiful, often fail to mirror the permanence of a significant life milestone.

From our signature birthday collections to our grand anniversary roses, we curate every arrangement in our master atelier in Business Bay, Dubai. By sourcing the world's finest roses and applying a proprietary bio-based preservation process, we ensure they remain in a state of perpetual bloom for over 365 days.

Philosophy of the Founder

Venus Fleur was conceptualized by a Dubai-based floral architect with a decade of experience in the international luxury market. The philosophy is simple: A gift is a communication of value. If a relationship is meant to last, the gift should reflect that intention.

This "Endurance Philosophy" guides every decision we make—from the selection of our bio-based solutions to the hand-written calligraphy on our signature stationery. Today, our founder's vision is represented in the homes of Palm Jumeirah and the executive suites of DIFC.

Venus Fleur Integrity FAQ

Where is the Venus Fleur atelier located?

Our main creative hub is located in Business Bay, Dubai. This central location allows us to coordinate elite same-day deliveries to Downtown, Palm Jumeirah, and DIFC with unmatched punctuality.

How long has Venus Fleur been operating?

Established in 2021, Venus Fleur has quickly become the gold standard for preserved floral luxury in Dubai, catering to the city's most prestigious districts and corporate entities.

Do you offer international shipping from Dubai?

While our primary concierge service is focused on the UAE, we offer bespoke international shipping for our signature rose boxes upon special request to ensure our luxury reaches you anywhere in the world.

Crafting Eternity

Born in the heart of Dubai, Venus Fleur was founded on a simple yet profound belief: that luxury gifting should be as enduring as the moments it celebrates.

We specialize in the curation of premium preserved roses, sourced from the finest gardens and treated with a specialized process that allows them to maintain their natural beauty for over a year. Our mission is to provide Dubai's elite with a floral experience that transcends the ordinary.

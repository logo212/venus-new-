Flower Delivery FAQ

What is the deadline for same-day delivery?

To guarantee same-day arrival, please place your order before 6:00 PM GST. For 2-hour express requests, contact our concierge via WhatsApp.

Is delivery packaging discreet for surprises?

Yes. Every surprise is delivered in unmarked premium carrying bags to ensure the reveal remains a total mystery until the moment of presentation.

Do you offer climate-controlled transport?

Absolutely. All deliveries utilize climate-stabilized vehicles to protect the texture and longevity of your preserved roses during the Dubai heat.

Is delivery available outside of Dubai?

Yes, we offer scheduled next-day delivery to Abu Dhabi and Sharjah for our signature collections. Contact us for specific timing and availability.

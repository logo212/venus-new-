Choosing by Year: An Expert Gifting Guide

Our floral designers in Business Bay have curated a guide to help you choose the right arrangement based on the milestone you are celebrating:

The 1st Anniversary: The Paper Year

The first year is about new beginnings. While tradition suggests paper, modern luxury in Dubai leans towards White or Blush Eternal Roses. They represent the purity of a new marriage and the bright future ahead. Delivering a "Single Eternal Rose" box is a popular choice for young couples in Dubai Marina.

The 5th Anniversary: The Wood Year

Five years represent roots and stability. Our larger "Square Velvet Collection" in rich burgundy or deep red is the preferred choice for this milestone. It signals that your love has matured and grown into something substantial.

The 10th & 20th Anniversaries: Decades of Devotion

For a decade of marriage, the gesture must be grand. This is where our Gold-dipped roses or the "Grand Round Collection" (featuring 50+ roses) take center stage. These are frequently delivered to private celebrations in Jumeirah Bay Island and the Bulgari Resort.

Luxury Redefined: Bespoke Wedding Florals for Dubai's Elite

A wedding in Dubai is not just an event; it is a masterpiece of logistics, design, and unparalleled luxury. From the iconic silhouette of the Burj Al Arab to the modern grandeur of Atlantis The Royal, every wedding deserves a floral language that speaks of eternal commitment. At Venus Fleur, we translate your love story into breathtaking floral realities.

Our specialty lies in the fusion of high-end bridal artistry and the practical demands of the UAE climate. Traditional fresh flowers often struggle under the golden sun of Palm Jumeirah or the intense indoor lighting of grand ballrooms. Our signature preserved eternal roses offer the perfect solution.

Your bridal bouquet is the most intimate part of your wedding decor. Our master florists in Business Bay curate each bouquet to match the bride's silhouette and gown. By using eternal roses, your bouquet becomes a permanent keepsake of your special day. Imagine your wedding bouquet sitting perfectly in your Dubai Hills home a year later.

Wedding Floral FAQ

Which venues do you serve in Dubai?

We serve all major venues, including Burj Al Arab, Atlantis The Royal, Four Seasons Jumeirah, and private residences in Palm Jumeirah and Emirates Hills.

Can I use preserved roses for my bouquet?

Absolutely. Preserved roses are heat-resistant and will stay perfect for the entire wedding day and for over a year afterwards as a keepsake.

Do you offer custom floral installations for events?

Yes, Venus Fleur specializes in grand floral walls, table centerpieces, and bespoke eternal rose installations tailored to your wedding theme.

How far in advance should I book?

For grand weddings, we recommend booking a consultation at least 6-8 weeks in advance to ensure personalized floral curation and venue site visits.

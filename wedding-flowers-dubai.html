Wedding Floral FAQ

Do you provide floral decor for wedding venues in Dubai?

Yes, we offer full-service floral design for venues across Dubai, including hotels, private villas, and outdoor desert ceremonies.

Can I use preserved roses for my bridal bouquet?

Absolutely. Preserved roses are a popular choice for Dubai brides as they remain perfect in the heat and serve as a beautiful keepsake after the wedding.
